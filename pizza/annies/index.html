Our Story

Neighborhood Spot, Big Flavor

Annie's Pizzeria is the kind of place you grow up with. The place you hit after a long day, post-game, or on the nights you just can’t be bothered to cook. Simple ingredients, good dough, hot ovens, and people who care about getting your order right.

Stop in for a quick slice at the counter or take a pie home to the couch. Either way, we’re proud to be part of your regular rotation.
