Our Story

Welcome to
Taste of Italy

Located in the vibrant heart of Corona, Queens, Taste of Italy Restaurant & Pizzeria NYC brings you authentic Italian cuisine crafted with passion and tradition. We're proud to serve our community with dishes that celebrate the rich flavors of Italy.

From our hand-tossed pizzas to our homemade pasta dishes, every meal is prepared with the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes. Our kitchen combines traditional Italian cooking methods with a commitment to quality that you can taste in every bite.

Whether you're joining us for a family dinner, ordering delivery, or catering your special event, we're dedicated to providing you with an exceptional Italian dining experience. Come taste the difference that authentic Italian cooking makes.
