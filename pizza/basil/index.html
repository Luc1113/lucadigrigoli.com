About Us

Welcome to Burrata Basil Pizza, a vibrant eatery in the heart of New York City's iconic East Village. We've quickly become a favorite among locals and visitors alike, known for our commitment to authentic Italian pizza crafted with fresh ingredients and traditional techniques.

Our concept is simple: create the perfect pizza through quality ingredients, homemade sauces, and our signature dough that delivers the ideal balance of crispy and chewy. Every pizza is a celebration of flavors designed to delight pizza lovers everywhere.

What We Offer

  • Artisanal Pizzas: Crafted with fresh, quality ingredients
  • Traditional Techniques: Authentic Italian pizza-making methods
  • Homemade Sauces: Made fresh daily in our kitchen
  • Signature Dough: Perfectly crispy and chewy every time
  • Gluten-Free Options: Available for our guests with dietary needs
  • Casual Yet Inviting: Perfect for any occasion
