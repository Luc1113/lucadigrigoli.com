Story

Dough made daily, sauce simmered slow.

We start every morning with our naturally leavened dough, crushed tomato sauce, and whole milk mozzarella. Every pizza is stretched by hand and finished in a stone hearth oven.

  • Stone-fired crust with a crisp edge and soft center.
  • Seasonal vegetables, house meatballs, and imported cheeses.
  • Family-style trays, party packages, and easy catering pickup.
