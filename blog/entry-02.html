There's Kinda A Lot Going On

category: life updates

date: 16th March 2026, morning.

Please keep your hands and arms INSIDE the blog post at all times.

We're doing this again?! Holy consistency!

Sure, something like that. It's Monday, the weather's nice (joy), and as the title states there's kinda a lot going on. Let's talk about it. But first:

Apparently, my deepest darkest secret is this new album at the rate I'm going. Is that a non-answer? Sure, but I did say I'd never tell, Esmeralda (Shoutout as promised, by the way)! Here's a secret of mine: When I was younger, I used to have an irrational fear of The Incredibles (yes, the Disney one). Have fun with that knowledge.

I'll start with some news. Recording has been a bit of a non-process lately, at least on the vocal front. I've been noticing some vocal fatigue when I sit for sessions as of late, and I'm getting it checked by an ENT out on the 25th. I hope to hear that there's no issues going on with my precious vocal folds, but of course if there's something going on I'll need to adjust accordingly.

Of course, the weather's changing and anything could affect anything, so more on that as it develops. Best case, I hear that all's fine and I just ease myself back into things.

This has been, however, a good time to crank out some instrumental recordings, so momentum isn't at a complete stop! I finally got the electric guitar (and bass, which is just a guitar tuned to drop D) down for "Broken Up" which was nagging at me for a while!

Anything else new?

I've also been improving my design skills! That's super fun. I'm learning how to design and I started this 100 day UI/UX challenge to force my way into googling a lot of design stuff. I'm nowhere near good at it yet, but this website could eventually get another makeover because of it!
