Zzzzzz, Honk Mimimimimi

category: music / life / yapping

date: 12th May 2026, Tuesday

As Gordon of Radiobed once famously said: “I’m asleep… I’m a snoozer… Why the hell am I sleeping here? I’m on the sidewalk.”

Hello once more, dearest readers.

It’s Tuesday? Again?

It is! That’s for two reasons:

A. I’ve been soooooooooo tired lately. It’s chronic. Sleep schedules are weird and my work time got moved, my body’s adjusting to that.

B. NEW MUSIC TODAY WOOOOO

Savannah

There was a time last year where I’d wake up and barely remember I had an appointment with the University Counselling Center. I’d rush out of my apartment into my car, park illegally someplace (because God knows finding parking at Binghamton University was a nightmare), pop my hazards on, and rush into my appointment a few minutes late.

Every single time, Savannah, my counselor at the time, would be there ready and waiting to ask just where I was able to dump my car this time. It was wonderful. In a world where she could have dropped me for my consistent lateness, she fought for me and met me where I was at with understanding. I did my best to show up on time. She got me through one of the toughest times I’ve been through yet.

Thank you, Savannah. Though I’m unsure you’ll ever hear it, this song’s for you. I appreciate everything. :)

Music Updates!

Yours truly is getting ready for the pilot of the new television extravaganza, “THE KISS AND TELL SHOW!” I’m totally not nervous about getting closer to the fabled second album, whattttt who said that???

It’s kinda been a myth since forever, and I want to really make this one count. The EP is my first step towards doing that! The tracklist as of right now for the EP goes as follows:

  1. Sorry To You
  2. What's Going On?
  3. Pretend
  4. Alone Again
  5. Broken Up
  6. Kiss & Tell
  7. Sorry To You (Voice Memo)

I’m really excited about these songs. They mean a lot to me and I consider them some of my best songwriting. In there I might sprinkle a little Ricky Jamaraz kinda meta fun, because since “Worst Album Ever” changed my life I’ve longed to kinda have an aesthetic like that in an album. It’s an experiment! It’s an EP! I’m gonna need all the help I can get in spreading the word so when that time comes PLEEEASSEEEE listen and tell others to do the same!

here is the pinterest board for the EP so you can get an idea of the vibe if you're interested!

More on this soon.

What else is up?

Settling into my summer rhythm! The weather’s getting nicer! Open mics start up again at The Cup Coffeehouse in Wantagh, locals should totally pull up and enjoy some music from some insanely talented people. I’m gonna see if I can even host some of them, because that sounds like a lot of fun.

It helped me get out of my shell about being public with my music again, and I even wrote my 100th song a day there on the spot! The crowd rocks, I can’t wait to see my friends Eddie, Silvia, Mitch, Anna, and many others back there.

They’re restarting their sticker table there because they replaced all the furniture, and I’m totally adding at least one sticker to it. Cmon. How do you not. Still not done with my Night in the Woods replay, but close to ending it. Review coming soon, promise!
