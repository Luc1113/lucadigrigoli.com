Why did I make this?

I made this because I believe having a website to showcase my work is important. That being said, I refuse to have this made for me by HTML templates or Wix.com or something. As a computer science major, I made the decision that I'd learn how to make a website for myself. That's why, at least for now, this website is riddled with buttons and dropdowns that don't work.
Thanks for being here, I hope eventually to make something super awesome!
