Our Story

Since 1985, Mario's Pizza has been serving authentic, handcrafted pizzas to the Oceanside community. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved local landmark.

We use only the finest ingredients: imported Italian tomatoes, fresh mozzarella made daily, and our secret family recipe dough that's been passed down through three generations. Every pizza is made with love and baked to perfection in our traditional brick oven.

Come taste the difference that passion and tradition make!
